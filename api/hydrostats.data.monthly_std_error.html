monthly_std_error¶

hydrostats.data.monthly_std_error(merged_data)[source]¶

Takes a dataframe with an index of datetime type and both recorded and predicted streamflow values and calculates the monthly standard error of the streamflow over the course of the data. Returns a dataframe with the date format as MM in the index and the two columns of the data’s standard error
