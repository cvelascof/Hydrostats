Welcome to hydrostats’s documentation!¶

Hydrostats is a library of tools and functions for users working with timeseries data. It has tools for preprocessing data, visualizing data, and for calculating error metrics on observed and predicted time series. It has over 60 error metrics, with many metrics specific to the field of hydrology.
